## What is a CMS?

### The Simple Explanation

A **Content Management System (CMS)** is software that lets you manage your website content without writing code.

**Think of it like:**
- Microsoft Word for your website
- A dashboard where you can edit everything
- Your personal website control center

### The Technical Explanation

A CMS separates your website's content (text, images, data) from its presentation (design, layout, code). This allows:

- **Content editors** to update content through a user-friendly interface
- **Developers** to build features without touching content
- **Designers** to change styling without affecting content
- **Marketers** to optimize and test without IT dependency

**Role Examples:**

- Full access to everything
- Can manage users
- Can change settings

- Can edit content

- Can see content

**Advanced Permission Features:**

**Client Value:**
